Why do you love seals so much?

I'm not sure I can tell you exactly where seal mania started, just that it is here.
To be honest, I never knew much about seals for the majority of my life, as most people do. The only seal content i had ever seen was this video of an elephant seal on YouTube many years ago.
However, in 2022 I saw this image posted on the internet, and became very curious about the original video, by seal photographer Rei Ohara.
Since then, I have become more and more enamored with seals every day, wanting to learn more about them and how they live, regardless of age or species.
My love of seals has led to me making many friends both online and offline, and has helped me gain confidence to be who I am and speak on what I want to without fear, so I am eternally grateful.
I hope this website encourages others to appreciate the humble seal, and gives some explanation as to why I am the way I am.
